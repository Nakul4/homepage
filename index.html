About Me

Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Nakul D Ghate. At present, I am a final year undergraduate student at IIT Bombay, India. My specialization lies in Mechanical engineering. Currently I am involved and working in novel technologies in Additive Manufacturing and its applications. My present work includes improvements in biomedical implants technology and fabrication.

I belong to Gwalior which lies in the northern area of Madhya Pradesh. It is a beautiful city surrounded by mountains on all four sides and Tighara Dam in the south-west region.

University of Montreal, QC, Canada

  • Solidification of falling droplet
    • Simulated solidification of falling droplet on a cold flat surface at cryogenic temperatures
    • Multiphase flow field model was incorporated to analyze the droplet and surrounding fluid movement
  • Directional solidification of fluids
    • Solved Stefan's problem of heat transfer, fluid flow and phase transition inside a partially heated square cavity
    • Simulated uni-directional and bi-directional melting of ice using 2D LBM scheme

World Congress of Micro and NanoManufacturing           2018

4M conference, Portoroz, Slovenia

Presented my work on "Laser surface modification (LSM) of titanium alloy" amongst people from over 100 universities. The work was primarily based on improvements in implants surface using laser processing.

Along with the experimental analysis, I also simulated the LSM process and generated the resulting surface topologies with various process parameters. The final goal was to determine the optimum process parameters for fabrication of particular implant.
